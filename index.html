ABOUT US

Welcome to "Burger Bun", where the art of crafting exceptional burgers takes center stage in every bite. Our restaurant is a haven for burger enthusiasts, offering a diverse menu that transcends traditional expectations. At Burger Bun, we believe in elevating the humble burger to a gourmet experience. Our culinary artisans meticulously source the finest, locally-sourced ingredients to create a symphony of flavors and textures that redefine the burger landscape. From burger bun to sandwich burgers, each burger is a masterpiece, thoughtfully assembled to satisfy discerning palates. Our commitment to quality extends beyond the ingredients; it permeates our welcoming atmosphere and dedicated service. Whether you're a classic burger connoisseur or an adventurous taste explorer, Burger Bun invites you to savor a symphony of flavors in every bite. Join us in celebrating the simplicity and indulgence of the perfect burger – a true culinary delight awaits you at Burger Bun.

OUR MASTER CHEFS

Sanjeev Kapoor

Sanjeev Kapoor is one of India’s most renowned and influential chefs, often referred to as the “Chef of the Nation”. Sanjeev Kapoor remains an influential figure in the culinary world, and his impact on Indian cuisine and cooking enthusiasts continues to be significant.

Ranveer Brar

Ranveer Brar is a talented and popular Indian chef, television personality, and author. He remains a prominent figure in the Indian culinary scene, and his influence continues to grow through his television appearances, cookbooks, and culinary ventures.

Vikas Khanna

Vikas Khanna is a renowned Indian-American chef, restaurateur, cookbook author, filmmaker, and humanitarian. Khanna’s cookbooks, which were published and got positive reviews and an extensive readership, were the catalyst for his journey to global fame.
